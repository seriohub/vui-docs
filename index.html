VUI Simplify Your Velero Backups Management

Your all-in-one dashboard for Velero backups

Introduction

Intuitive Dashboard

Access all your Velero backup information in one centralized, user-friendly dashboard. Monitor backups, restores, and system health at a glance.

Real-Time Alerts and Notifications

Stay informed with real-time alerts and notifications about the status of your backups. Receive updates via email, SMS, or in-app notifications.

Cron Heat Map Visualization

Visualize your backup activity with heat map. Identify peak usage times and optimize your backup strategies.

Centralized Management for Multiple Clusters

Effortlessly manage and monitor backups across all your clusters from one centralized interface.

Simplified Storage Class Mapping

Easily map and manage storage classes across different environments. Simplify storage management and ensure consistency in your backup and restore processes.

S3 Repository Statistics and Restic Operations

Access statistics directly from your S3 repositories. Monitor storage usage. Perform advanced operations on Restic repositories.
